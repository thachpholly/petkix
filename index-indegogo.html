Our 4-legged friends need us around to be happy and cheerful, which is why pet cameras at home are helpful. These are great ways to watch over, play, train, feed and simply take care of them the best that we can even when we aren’t available.

Petkix is the world's only AI pet camera that lets dogs and cats control it.

When Petkix's AI mode is turned on, your paws can control Petkix by their signals. You can customize the triggers as well. For example:


PETKIX at CES 

               

Features

Smart Selfie Detection:

 Selfies are not just for people. With Petkix, your dog and cat can take and send a selfie whenever they want. You also get a selfie alert on your smartphone.

Smart Treat Tossing:

 Every pet loves a treat. Petkix detects when your pet wants some treats and toss them using the Dynamic Treat Tossing feature.

Laser Pointer & Game:

 You can also help your paws find their treats easily in manual mode

 

 Or Petkix can play with your cats in autoplay mode.

Bluetooth Speaker:

 Nothing works as well as music to soothe your dogs and cats, so Petkix allows you to play your favorite Spotify songs for your dear pets, or let your dogs and cats turn it on themselves.

Portability:​

Petkix works great with any USB power bank. It also offers 36 hours of continuous usage with a 20000 mAh power bank

 

 

When our pets are happy, we are happy too. And when we are happy, we’re able to make a meaningful impact on the world. So let's make the world a paradise for our pets.
